Born in 2008

There are too many exciting things to tell about

our history, so here is a small and simple timeline.

  • 2010

    StarCraft II

    Before the release of the game we created (and then maintained) an Italian fansite containing info, FAQ, guides, articles and downloadable content.

  • 2010

    Bone Crushers

    A new World of Warcraft guild was born in the EU server Anachronos (Alliance) mainly focused on PVE, bringing together different members from Vanilla to WotLK.

  • 2012

    Hitbox

    During our journey we have decided to adopt a more professional approach to the events we manage by founding Hitbox.it furthermore, with the new HBX.gg teams, we have expanded our international eSports presence.

  • 2013

    Titanfall

    Our biggest side-project about a single video game title, the first Italian community of Titanfall officially recognized by Respawn Entertainment.

  • 2014

    Evolve

    We made the Italian community of Evolve (a game by 2K Games), managing several fan pages and groups on different social networks.

  • 2014

    Overwatched

    With Blizzard's newly announced game "Overwatch" we released a news aggregator website along with a small but dedicated community.
